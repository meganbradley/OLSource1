# Service Map Macros
These macros define service maps and entries.  
  
|||  
|-|-|  
|[BEGIN_SERVICE_MAP](../VS_csharp/begin_service_map.md)|Marks the beginning of an ATL service map.|  
|[END_SERVICE_MAP](../VS_csharp/end_service_map.md)|Marks the end of an ATL service map.|  
|[SERVICE_ENTRY](../VS_csharp/service_entry.md)|Indicates that the object supports a specific service ID.|  
|[SERVICE_ENTRY_CHAIN](../VS_csharp/service_entry_chain.md)|Instructs [IServiceProviderImpl::QueryService](../VS_csharp/iserviceproviderimpl--queryservice.md) to chain to the specified object.|
