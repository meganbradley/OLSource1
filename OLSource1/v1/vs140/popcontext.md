# POPCONTEXT
Restores part or all of the current <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> (saved by the [PUSHCONTEXT](../vs140/pushcontext.md) directive). The <CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> can be **ASSUMES**, <CodeContentPlaceHolder>3\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>, **LISTING**, **CPU**, or **ALL**.  
  
## Syntax  
  
<CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>
