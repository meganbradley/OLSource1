# CMFCButton::m_bRightImage
Indicates whether to display an image on the right side of the button.  
  
## Syntax  
  
<CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>  
## Remarks  
 Set the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> member to <CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> to specify that the framework will display the button's image to the right of the button's text label.  
  
## Requirements  
 **Header:** afxbutton.h
