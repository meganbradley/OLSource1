# Method declaration statements must be the first on a logical line
A <CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> or <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> statement follows a colon (:) statement separator. <CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> or <CodeContentPlaceHolder>3\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> must be the only statement on its source line.  
  
 **Error ID:** BC32009  
  
### To correct this error  
  
-   Separate the multiple statements onto different lines.
