# &#39;AddHandler&#39; and &#39;RemoveHandler&#39; method parameters cannot be declared &#39;ByRef&#39;
The parameters of the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> and <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> methods cannot be declared with the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> modifier.  
  
 **Error ID:** BC31134  
  
### To correct this error  
  
-   Remove the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>3\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> keyword from the parameter.
