# Compiler Error C2446
'operator' : no conversion from 'type1' to 'type2'  
  
 The compiler cannot convert <CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> to <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>. The conversion may not make sense because it violates C/C++ semantics.
