# &#39;Shared&#39; cannot be combined with &#39;&lt;specifier&gt;&#39; on a property declaration
You have attempted to combine the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> modifier with a specifier that is not valid in such a combination on a property declaration.  
  
 **Error ID:** BC30502  
  
### To correct this error  
  
-   Remove the specifier.
