# Cannot delete a registry hive
An attempt has been made to delete a registry hive. Hives are top-level registry keys, such as <CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> and <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>, and cannot be deleted.  
  
### To correct this error  
  
-   Check the registry key you are attempting to delete, and make sure you are supplying the correct path.
