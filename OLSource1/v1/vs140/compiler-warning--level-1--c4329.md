# Compiler Warning (level 1) C4329
__declspec(align()) is ignored on enum  
  
 Use of the [align](../vs140/align--c---.md) keyword of the [__declspec](../vs140/__declspec.md) modifier is not allowed on an <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>. The following sample generates C4329:  
  
<CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>
