# CAutoHideDockSite::m_nExtraSpace
The size of the space between the edges of the [CAutoHideDockSite Class](../vs140/cautohidedocksite-class.md) and the [CMFCAutoHideBar Class](../vs140/cmfcautohidebar-class.md) objects.  
  
## Syntax  
  
<CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>  
## Remarks  
 When a <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> is docked at a <CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>, it should not occupy the whole dock site. This global variable controls the extra space between the left or top border of the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>3\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> and the corresponding <CodeContentPlaceHolder>4\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> edge. Whether the top or left edge is used depends on the current alignment.  
  
## Requirements  
 **Header:** afxautohidedocksite.h
