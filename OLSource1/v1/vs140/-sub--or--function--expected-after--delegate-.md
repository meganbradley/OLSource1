# &#39;Sub&#39; or &#39;Function&#39; expected after &#39;Delegate&#39;
A <CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> statement does not specify a <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> or <CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> procedure. The <CodeContentPlaceHolder>3\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> or <CodeContentPlaceHolder>4\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> keyword must immediately follow the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>5\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> keyword.  
  
 **Error ID:** BC30278  
  
### To correct this error  
  
1.  Add the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>6\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> or <CodeContentPlaceHolder>7\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> keyword immediately after <CodeContentPlaceHolder>8\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>.  
  
2.  Specify a procedure name, argument list, and return type as appropriate.
