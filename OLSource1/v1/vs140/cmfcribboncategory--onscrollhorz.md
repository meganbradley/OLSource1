# CMFCRibbonCategory::OnScrollHorz
Scrolls the ribbon category in the horizontal direction.

 [in] <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>  
 <CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> to scroll to the left; <CodeContentPlaceHolder>3\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> to scroll to the right.
