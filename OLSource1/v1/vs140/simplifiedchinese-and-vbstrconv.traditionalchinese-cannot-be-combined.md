# SimplifiedChinese and VbStrConv.TraditionalChinese cannot be combined
Your application is attempting to combine the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> enumeration members <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> and <CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>, which are mutually exclusive.  
  
### To correct this error  
  
-   Remove either <CodeContentPlaceHolder>3\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> or <CodeContentPlaceHolder>4\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>.
