# CPrintInfo::m_bDocObject
Contains a flag indicating whether the document being printed is a DocObject.  
  
## Remarks  
 Data members <CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> and **m_nOffsetPage** are invalid unless this flag is **TRUE**.  
  
## Requirements  
 **Header:** afxext.h
