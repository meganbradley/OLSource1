## Remarks  
 The *origin* argument specifies the initial position and can be one of the following manifest constants:  
  
|Constant|Meaning|  
|--------------|-------------|  
|<CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>|End of file|  
|<CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>|Current position of file pointer|  
|<CodeContentPlaceHolder>3\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>|Beginning of file|
