# CMFCPopupMenu::SetRightAlign
Sets the menu alignment for pop-up menus.  
  
## Syntax  
  
<CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>  
#### Parameters  
 [in] <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>  
 A Boolean that indicates the menu alignment. <CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> indicates right alignment, <CodeContentPlaceHolder>3\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> indicates left alignment.  
  
## Remarks  
 By default, all pop-up menus are left-aligned.  
  
## Requirements  
 **Header:** afxpopupmenu.h
