# Compiler Warning (level 4) C4433
a finalizer must have protected accessibility; changing to protected access  
  
 C4433 indicates that the compiler changed the accessibility of a finalizer.  Finalizers must have protected accessibility. For more information, see [Destructors and Finalizers](../vs140/how-to--define-and-consume-classes-and-structs--c---cli-.md#BKMK_Destructors_and_finalizers).
