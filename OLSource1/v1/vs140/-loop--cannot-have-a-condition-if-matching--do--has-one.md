# &#39;Loop&#39; cannot have a condition if matching &#39;Do&#39; has one
A <CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> statement contains a <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> or <CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> clause, and the corresponding <CodeContentPlaceHolder>3\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> statement also contains such a clause. Only one of the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>4\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> and <CodeContentPlaceHolder>5\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> statements in a loop can specify a condition.  
  
 **Error ID:** BC30238  
  
### To correct this error  
  
-   Remove the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>6\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> or <CodeContentPlaceHolder>7\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> clause from either the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>8\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> statement or the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>9\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> statement.
