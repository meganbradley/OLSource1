# IDiaSegment::get_virtualAddress
Retrieves the virtual address (VA) of the beginning of the section.  
  
## Syntax  
  
<CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>  
#### Parameters  
 <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>
