# CMFCRibbonBar::IsQuickAccessToolbarOnTop
Indicates whether the quick access toolbar is located over or under the ribbon bar.  
  
## Syntax  
  
<CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>  
## Return Value  
 <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> if the quick access toolbar is located over the ribbon bar; <CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> if the quick access toolbar is located under the ribbon bar.  
  
## Requirements  
 **Header:** afxribbonbar.h
