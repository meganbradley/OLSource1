# CDacl::RemoveAllAces
Removes all of the ACEs (access-control entries) contained in the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> object.  
  
## Syntax  
  
<CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>  
## Remarks  
 Removes every **ACE** (access-control entry) structure (if any) in the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> object.  
  
## Requirements  
 **Header:** atlsecurity.h
