# &#39;End AddHandler&#39; must be preceded by a matching &#39;AddHandler&#39; declaration
An <CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> statement occurred without a corresponding <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> statement. <CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> must be preceded by a corresponding <CodeContentPlaceHolder>3\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> statement.  
  
 **Error ID:** BC31124  
  
### To correct this error  
  
-   Check that a preceding <CodeContentPlaceHolder>4\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> statement is valid and correctly spelled.
