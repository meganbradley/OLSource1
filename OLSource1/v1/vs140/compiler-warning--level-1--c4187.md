# Compiler Warning (level 1) C4187
\#import attributes 'attribute1' and 'attribute2' are incompatible; both ignored  
  
 A [#import](../vs140/sharpimport-directive--c---.md) statement specified `no_implementation` and `implementation_only` attributes. Both are ignored.
