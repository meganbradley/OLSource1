# C28216
warning C28216: The _Check_return\_ annotation only applies to post-conditions for the specific function parameter.  
  
 The <CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> annotation has been applied in a context other than <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>; it may need a <CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> (or <CodeContentPlaceHolder>3\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>) modifier, or it may be placed incorrectly.
