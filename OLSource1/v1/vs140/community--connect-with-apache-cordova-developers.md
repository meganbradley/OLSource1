# Community: Connect with Apache Cordova Developers
[!INCLUDE[cordova_disclaimer](../vs140/includes/cordova_disclaimer_md.md)]  
  
 We welcome your feedback and your involvement in the Visual Studio community. We encourage you to connect with other developers who are creating apps using Visual Studio Tools for Apache Cordova. Here are some of the resources for posting questions and sharing answers, ideas, and comments.  
  
-   Find answers to common questions in our [FAQ](http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?LinkID=398476).  
  
-   Find a list of current bugs and fixes on the [Known Issues](http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?LinkID=398782) page.  
  
-   If you have other questions or bugs, report them on ![StackOverflow logo](../vs140/media/cordova_stackoverflow.png "Cordova_StackOverflow")[StackOverflow](http://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/visual-studio-cordova). We monitor the site regularly and respond to your questions and issues.  
  
-   For product suggestions, email us at [vscordovatools@microsoft.com](mailto:%20vscordovatools@microsoft.com).  
  
-   Follow us on ![Twitter logo](../vs140/media/cordova_twitter.png "Cordova_Twitter") Twitter [@VSCordovaTools](https://twitter.com/VSCordovaTools).  
  
-   Contribute to our [community documentation](https://github.com/Microsoft/cordova-docs) and [samples](https://github.com/Microsoft/cordova-samples)
