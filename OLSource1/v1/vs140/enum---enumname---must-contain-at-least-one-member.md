# Enum &#39;&lt;enumname&gt;&#39; must contain at least one member
An empty enumeration occurs. At least one member must be declared between the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> statement and the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> statement.  
  
 **Error ID:** BC30280  
  
### To correct this error  
  
1.  Add a member declaration to the enumeration.
