# Specifiers valid only at the beginning of a declaration
Keywords specifying accessibility and other designations must appear before the declaration keyword, such as <CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> or <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>.  
  
 **Error ID:** BC30181  
  
### To correct this error  
  
-   Move all specifiers between the attribute list (if any) and the declaration keyword.
