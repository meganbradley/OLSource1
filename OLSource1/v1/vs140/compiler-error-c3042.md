# Compiler Error C3042
'copyprivate' and 'nowait' clauses cannot appear together on OpenMP 'directive' directive  
  
 The [copyprivate](../vs140/copyprivate.md) and [nowait](../vs140/nowait.md) clauses are mutually exclusive on the specified directive. To fix this error, remove one or both of the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> or <CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> clauses.  
  
 The following sample generates C3042:  
  
<CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>
