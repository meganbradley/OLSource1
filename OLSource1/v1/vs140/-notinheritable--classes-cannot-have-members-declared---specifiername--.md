# &#39;NotInheritable&#39; classes cannot have members declared &#39;&lt;specifiername&gt;&#39;
Override modifiers cannot be used with <CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> classes because their members cannot be overridden.  
  
 **Error ID:** BC30607  
  
### To correct this error  
  
-   Remove override modifiers, such as <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>, <CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>, or <CodeContentPlaceHolder>3\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>, from the class definition.
