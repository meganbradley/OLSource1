# Compiler Warning (level 1) C4077
unknown check_stack option  
  
 The old form of the **check_stack** pragma is used with an unknown argument. The argument must be <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>, <CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>, <CodeContentPlaceHolder>3\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>, <CodeContentPlaceHolder>4\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>, or empty.  
  
 The compiler ignores the pragma and leaves the stack checking unchanged.
