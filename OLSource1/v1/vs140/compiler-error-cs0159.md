# Compiler Error CS0159
No such label 'label' within the scope of the goto statement  
  
 The label referenced by the [goto](../vs140/goto--csharp-reference-.md) statement could not be found within the scope of the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> statement.  
  
 The following sample generates CS0159:  
  
<CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>
