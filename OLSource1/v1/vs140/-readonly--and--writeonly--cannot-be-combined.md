# &#39;ReadOnly&#39; and &#39;WriteOnly&#39; cannot be combined
The <CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> and <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> modifiers are mutually exclusive.  
  
 **Error ID:** BC30635  
  
### To correct this error  
  
-   Specify either <CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> or <CodeContentPlaceHolder>3\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>, but not both.
