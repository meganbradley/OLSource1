# &#39;Shared&#39; cannot be combined with &#39;&lt;specifier&gt;&#39; on a method declaration
You have attempted to combine the <CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> modifier with a specifier, such as <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>, <CodeContentPlaceHolder>2\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>, or <CodeContentPlaceHolder>3\</CodeContentPlaceHolder>, that is not valid in such a combination on a method declaration.  
  
 **Error ID:** BC30501  
  
### To correct this error  
  
-   Remove the specifier.
