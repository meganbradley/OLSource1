# Classes cannot be declared &#39;&lt;specifier&gt;&#39;
You have attempted to declare a <CodeContentPlaceHolder>0\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> or <CodeContentPlaceHolder>1\</CodeContentPlaceHolder> with an invalid specifier.  
  
 **Error ID:** BC30461  
  
### To correct this error  
  
-   Remove the specifier.
