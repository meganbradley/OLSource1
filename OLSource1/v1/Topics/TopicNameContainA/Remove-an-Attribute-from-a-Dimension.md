# Remove an Attribute from a Dimension
  To remove an attribute from a dimension in [!INCLUDE[msCoName](../../Token\Other/msCoName_md.md)] [!INCLUDE[ssNoVersion](../../Token\Other/ssNoVersion_md.md)] [!INCLUDE[ssASnoversion](../../Token\Other/ssASnoversion_md.md)], right\-click the attribute in the **Attributes** pane of the **Dimension Structure** tab of Dimension Designer in [!INCLUDE[ssBIDevStudioFull](../../Token\Other/ssBIDevStudioFull_md.md)], and then click **Delete**. In the **Delete Objects** dialog box, click **OK**. This removes the attribute from the dimension, but does not affect the data source view for the dimension.
