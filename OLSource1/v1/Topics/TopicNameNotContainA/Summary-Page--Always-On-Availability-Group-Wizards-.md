# Summary Page (Always On Availability Group Wizards)
<a name="Top"></a>  
  
  This help topic describes the options of the **Summary** page. This topic applies to the [!INCLUDE[ssAoNewAgWiz](../../Topics/TopicNameNotContainA/includes/ssAoNewAgWiz_md.md)], [!INCLUDE[ssAoAddRepWiz](../../Topics/TopicNameNotContainA/includes/ssAoAddRepWiz_md.md)], [!INCLUDE[ssAoAddDbWiz](../../Topics/TopicNameNotContainA/includes/ssAoAddDbWiz_md.md)] and [!INCLUDE[ssAoFoAgWiz](../../Topics/TopicNameNotContainA/includes/ssAoFoAgWiz_md.md)] of [!INCLUDE[ssCurrent](../../Topics/TopicNameContainA/includes/ssCurrent_md.md)]. Use the grid on this page to review your choices for the new availability group. To make one or more changes, click **Previous** to return to the relevant page or pages. When you are ready, click **Next** to return to the **Summary** page. Once you are satisfied with your choices, click **Finish**.  
  
##  <a name="PageOptions"></a> Summary Page Options  
 **Script**  
 Click to generate a [!INCLUDE[tsql](../../Topics/TopicNameContainA/includes/tsql_md.md)] script for the actions listed in the summary grid. You will be prompted to specify a destination for the script.  
  
 **Previous**  
 Click to return to the page immediately preceding the current page. You can use the **Previous** button to navigate backward to any of the preceding pages and, optionally, change any of your specified values.  
  
 **Finish**  
 Once you are satisfied with your choices, click to make the wizard proceed with creating the availability group.  
  
 **Cancel**  
 Click to cancel the wizard. On the **Summary** page, cancelling the wizard causes it to exit without performing any actions.
