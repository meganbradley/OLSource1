# Enable Dimension Writeback (Dimension) (Business Intelligence Wizard)
Use the **Enable Dimension Writeback** page to select a dimension for which the Business Intelligence Wizard will enable writeback.  
  
> [!NOTE]  
>  This page does not appear if the Business Intelligence Wizard was started from Dimension Designer or by right-clicking a dimension in Solution Explorer in [!INCLUDE[ssBIDevStudioFull](../../Topics/TopicNameContainA/includes/ssBIDevStudioFull_md.md)].  
  
## Options  
 **Dimension**  
 Select the dimension for which the wizard will enable writeback.
