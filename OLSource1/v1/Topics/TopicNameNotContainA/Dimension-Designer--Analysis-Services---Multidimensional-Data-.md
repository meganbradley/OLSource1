# Dimension Designer (Analysis Services - Multidimensional Data)
  Use Dimension Designer in [!INCLUDE[ssBIDevStudioFull](../../Token\Other/ssBIDevStudioFull_md.md)] to browse the data that the dimension contains, and to modify various properties of an existing dimension: attributes, hierarchies, levels, attribute relationships, and translations.  
  
 **To display Dimension Designer**  
  
-   In [!INCLUDE[ssBIDevStudioFull](../../Token\Other/ssBIDevStudioFull_md.md)], in **Solution Explorer**, use one of the following procedures procedures:  
  
    -   Right\-click a dimension and then click **Open** or **View Designer**.  
  
         —or—  
  
    -   Double\-click a dimension.  
  
## Pages  
 Dimension Designer contains the following pages:  
  
 [Dimension Structure](../Topic/Dimension%20Structure%20\(Dimension%20Designer\)%20\(Analysis%20Services%20-%20Multidimensional%20Data\).md)  
 Use this page to view and modify the attributes, hierarchies, and levels in the dimension.  
  
 [Attribute Relationships](../Topic/Attribute%20Relationships%20\(Dimension%20Designer\)%20\(Analysis%20Services%20-%20Multidimensional%20Data\).md)  
 Use thispage to create, modify or delete the attribute relationships of the selected dimension.  
  
 [Translations](../Topic/Translations%20\(Dimension%20Designer\)%20\(Analysis%20Services%20-%20Multidimensional%20Data\).md)  
 Use this page to view and modify translations associated with the dimension and its attributes, hierarchies, and levels.  
  
 [Browser](../Topic/Browser%20\(Dimension%20Designer\)%20\(Analysis%20Services%20-%20Multidimensional%20Data\).md)  
 Use this page to browse the members of a hierarchy that the dimension contains.
